This app allows you to find the missing person by matching the live image with image stored int its database. If the name comes over the image then that person matches with the one in the database otherwise the system would show unknown .

Face Recognition System



How to use?

Simply bring the person in front of the camera , if the name comes over then that person is in our database and you can contact us for the further paperwork. If no name comes up and you want us to add that person in our missin people list contact us and provide us the image of the person.
